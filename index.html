Even Our Building
Drinks Matcha

From the outside we're a small box on Murray Ave, wrapped top to bottom in green ivy that climbs a little higher every summer. You'll find us by the glow in the window at dusk.

Inside it's all soft light — Nelson bubble lamps overhead and a bar carved from backlit onyx that burns like a low sun. We pour single-origin espresso, brew pour over to order, and yes, the matcha keeps selling out. Again.

Pull up a blue chair and stay a while. We built this room to be lingered in — for the deep blue hours at either end of the day. Come early. Stay late.
